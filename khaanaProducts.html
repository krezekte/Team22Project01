About Our Offerings

Our products are all crafted using high quality, locally sourced ingredients from family-owned farms near Lafayette, Indiana. When you buy meals from Khaana, you know they are going to be healthy, delicious, and even better, fresh! On this page we have a list of all of our meals, as well as a list of our suppliers and some popular meals. You can choose any of these meals to be delivered to your doorstep.



From Farm to Your Table

  1. Proteins and produce are harvested at local farms. All of it is organic!
  2. When ingredients are received at our kitchens, we inspect them for freshness and quality.
  3. Meals are cooked, prepared, and carefully packaged for delivery.
  4. A Khaana box is delivered to your doorstep. All it takes is a few final steps and you have a healthy meal ready to eat!

Favorite Meals

Beef and Broccoli

Our Beef and Broccoli meal is a favorite for so many people! A nice warm meal prepared with organic beef, broccoli, mushrooms, and peas. The meal is cooked at our kitchen, so all you need to do at your home is heat it up and enjoy!




Chicken Salad

A classic salad is sometimes all you need for a delicious dinner! This chicken salad consists of lettuce, tomatoes, cucumber, and of course, organic chicken. This is a great light meal for a quick and easy dinner!




Hummus Wrap

This hummus wrap is great for a quick bite, lunch, or simple dinner. Chock full of organic carrots, cucumbers, lettuce, hummus, and other goodies, this meal is great for even the most health-conscious person. This meal also doesn't require heating, as it is best served chilled.




Spicy Chicken and Rice

Another fantastic meal for chicken lovers! This chicken dish is our most-liked spicy dish, it packs quite the punch. The chicken is served in a bowl of rice, which compliments the flavors perfectly! This meal is sure to delight all spice lovers.
