About Our Offerings

Our products are all crafted using high quality, locally sourced ingredients from family-owned farms near Lafayette, Indiana. When you buy meals from Khaana, you know they are going to be healthy, delicious, and even better, fresh! On this page we have a list of all of our meals, as well as a list of our suppliers and some popular meals. You can choose any of these meals to be delivered to your doorstep.



From Farm to Your Table

  1. Proteins and produce are harvested at local farms. All of it is organic!
  2. When ingredients are received at our kitchens, we inspect them for freshness and quality.
  3. Meals are cooked, prepared, and carefully packaged for delivery.
  4. A Khaana box is delivered to your doorstep. All it takes is a few final steps and you have a healthy meal ready to eat!
