About Us

Khaana is a modern foodservice company founded in 2015 in Indianapolis, Indiana. Our mission is to bring fresh, healthy, and locally-sourced meals to communities while supporting sustainable farming practices. We proudly work with trusted partners such as Fair Oaks Farms to deliver quality food to families, schools, universities, and businesses across the Midwest.
